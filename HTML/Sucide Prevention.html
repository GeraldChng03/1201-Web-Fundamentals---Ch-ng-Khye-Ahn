What is Self-Harm?

Self-Harm is the process of willingly:

  • Harm oneself.
  • Attempting Sucide.
  • Overdosing on substamces.

Self-Harm Facts

Every year....

  • 700,000 people end their lifes by committing sucide.
  • Additionally, for every of those successful sucide, there are 20 failed attempts.
  • 77% of these global sucides comes from lower and middle income countries.

In 2020, the United States alone have,

12.2 million considering sucide

3.2 million took a step further and planned their sucide

1.2 million attempted sucide

It is important that YOU get help or if YOU notice that your friends are troubled to help THEM.


Preventions for Self-Harm

You're not alone...

  • Recieve Help
  • Seek an appointment with a proffesional
  • Discuss troubling issues with family and freinds
  • Create a protective enviroment for all
  • Support those at risk and develop healthy and positive connections with everyone
  • Send us your concerns
