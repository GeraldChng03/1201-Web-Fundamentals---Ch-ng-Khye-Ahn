What is Anxiety?

An anxiety disorder is one type of a mental health condition. A person with this condition will experince and respond to certain situations with fear. Although everybody feels anxious at some points in their lives, people with anxiety disorder feel them more frequently and during times where you would feel normal. An anxiety disorder is beyond just feeling nervous or fearful in certain situations.

People may have anxiety disorder when:

  • Ability to function is altered or interfered.
  • Frequently overeacts in many situations.
  • Difficulty in containing emotions.

Risk Factors

Certain people are more likely to have or develop anxiety disorder. You may have a higher chance to develop anxiety disorders if you have or had:

  • Suffered PTSD(Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder) during your childhood.
  • Family history of anxiety or other mental health conditions.
  • Certain Physical Conditions such as: unusual heart rhythms (heart arrhythmias) or thyroid problems.

Anxiety Symptoms

People of any age, gender, race may experince some or all of the following symptomps. It is crucial to understand these symptomps to be able to differenate between weather you or your loved one has anxiety or depression.

Physical Symptoms:

  • Heart Palpitations
  • Nausea
  • Frquent muscle tensions
  • Shortness of Breath
  • Sweaty Hands
  • Diffculty breathing / Shortness of breath

Behavioral Symptoms:

  • Inability to remain calm
  • Diffculty with sleep

Mental Symptoms:

  • Feeling of uneasiness
  • Repeated sequencees of flashbacks
  • Obessive thoughts

Anxiety Causes

Causes

The causes of anxiety disorders are

  • Chemical imbalance
  • Consistenly and frequently stressed
  • Enviromental factors
  • Heredity

Treatments for Anxiety

Similar to other health problems, anxiety disorders requires attention and treatment to prevent further complications and issues from arrising. Please look at the helpline page to learn more and recieve professional medical support.

  • Medications such as Anti-anxiety medications, antidepressants, beta-blocker etc

    • These medications are not a pure cure for anxiety disorders, however, they do make you feel calmer in situations. They work by telling the brain to use varying amount of chemicals to help improve mood and reduce stress.

  • Therapy

Preventions for Anxiety

Anxiety disorders cannot be completely prevented. There is also no true cure for this condition. However, there are steps to aid people experincing this condition and can help reduce the symptoms you feel.

  • Live a healthy lifestyle

    • We recommend that you consume a balance and healthy diet; eat everything in moderation. It is also important to execrise regularly and have a healthy sleep pattern.

  • Limit caffiene
  • Seeking Proffesional Help
