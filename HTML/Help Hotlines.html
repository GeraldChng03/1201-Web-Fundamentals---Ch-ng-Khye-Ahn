How to recieve help

You're not alone in this fight...

  • Seek a professional help by visiting local General Practitioners (GP).

    • These professionals can provide a more detailed insight into your current conditions and struggles. They would be able to aid you much better as they can provide medications and advice tailored for you.

  • Discuss any troubling thoughts with your family and friends.

    • Discussing key issues with people you know will enable you to feel more understood and hence, more supported. This is crucial in your fight for a healthy mental health

  • Arrange a therapy/ one-on-one session with professionals.

Why getting help is important

Recieving help from professionals can significantly change your life:

  • Your chances of surviving increases dramatically, notably people with depression who undergo treatment recover 80%-90% of the time.
  • Eradicated or improved symptoms of mental health.
  • Prevents sucide.
  • Prevents certain physical conditions such as: aches, pain, etc.
  • Prevents serious conditions such as: heart attack, cancer, stroke, etc.
