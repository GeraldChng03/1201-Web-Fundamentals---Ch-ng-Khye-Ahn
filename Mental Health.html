Why Mental Health Is Important To You

Depression is a severe medical condition that affects your ability to function normally. It is considered as a mood disorders in the medical field and it carries a number of symptoms that affect people in their ocupation, education, social and mental life. Many who have depression suffer the inability to remember a happy or prideful past as they would be riddled with bad experinces. During a depressive episode, a person with depression often experience different thoughts than before they had a depressive episode. Thus, it makes an already diffuclt condition to be diagnosed much harder to diagnose.
